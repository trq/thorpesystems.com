Gentoo & more recently Funtoo have long been my GNU/Linux distributions of choice. Why? Not because of the performance or flexibility you inherently gain or from installing packages from source, but because you can (if you wish) use bleeding edge software. Take PHP for instance. I do allot of development in PHP and as such like to keep up to date with the latest goings on. PHP5.4rc1 was released 2 days ago, and while most distributions won't have a package available for at least six months after 5.4 is stable, Funtoo has a package available now. All you need do is unmask it. I setup an OpenVZ VM just to play with it, and the process was simple. 
$ sudo emerge --sync
$ echo 'PHP_TARGETS="php5-4"' | sudo tee -a /etc/make.conf
$ echo "=dev-lang/php-5.4.0_rc1" | sudo tee -a /etc/portage/package.unmask
$ sudo emerge php
Simple. 
$ php -v
